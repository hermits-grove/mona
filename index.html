mona
the transparent password manager
~
mona is a new kind of password manager.
one where you control your data.

owning your data matters.
  1. with mona you choose where your data is stored
  2. if mona fails, you still keep your data
mona does not have a custom backend.
mona is built on top of git.
  1. git gives us a foundation of a distributed multi-master database
  2. many popular git hosting services exist
